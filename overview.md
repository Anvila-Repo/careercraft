# Overview

## Purpose
CareerCraft is built to serve as an end-to-end companion for tech candidates at the entry and mid-levels. By breaking down the daunting, often opaque process of job searching into bite-sized, actionable milestones, CareerCraft transforms anxiety into a clear, structured roadmap to employment.

## Domain & Audience
- **Primary Domain**: Tech Job Search & Application Process (Software Engineering, QA, DevOps, Product, UI/UX Design, Data Science, Product Marketing).
- **Target Audience**: Entry-level graduates, self-taught developers, bootcamp grads, and mid-level professionals looking to pivot or level up their careers in technology.
